Introduction

FAHLogStats.NET is capable of monitoring tens or even hundreds of computers running the Folding@Home client from Stanford University. Folding@Home helps scientists investigate the causes and potential cures for:

  • Alzheimer's Disease
  • Cancer
  • Huntington's Disease
  • Osteogenisis Imperfecta
  • Parkinson's Disease

as well as analysing ribosomes and antibiotics.
